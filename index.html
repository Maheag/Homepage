Best places to Visit

Foz do Iguacu

The endless calm walks around this place are magic. You can feel and smell the breeze of the water in the air. This area is surrounded by trees, plants, shrubs, beautiful animals and insects. The village is full of friendly residents and it feels safe and welcoming. You can choose to stay in the village or camping nearby.

Lençois Maranhence

This is a unique place in the whole world. There are more then 85 natural-lagoons with clean, warm and blue water, it has spectacular powder sand dunes, where you can walk for hours or just relax sunbathing and swimming. This area is perfect for a romantic honeymoon.

Quixada Mountain hike and glide

This is a place for holidays full of adventures. You will have a choice of many sports: Cycling, walking, jogging, horse riding, hiking and, from the top of mountains, paragliding.
